Why does this matter?

NYC Local Law 97 of 2019

One of the most ambitious building emissions laws in the nation

In New York City, over two-thirds of all greenhouse gas emissions come from buildings. LL97 requires most buildings over 25,000 sq ft to meet new GHG emissions limits — with stricter limits taking effect from 2030.
